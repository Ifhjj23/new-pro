DHAN MATA SAHIB KAUR JI

She is the Divine Knowledge that is True, before, now and after; to what extent can I continue to praise Her?⁣

GURU PANTH KHALSA

Where the Khalsa is seated, call out 'Vahiguruji Ka Khalsa, Vahiguruji Ki Fateh'. Anyone, who looks upon the Khalsa with love, they then won't fall into ocean of existence again [they will be liberated].
